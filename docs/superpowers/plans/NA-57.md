**Goal:** Remove the standalone `/sdlc:adr` command entirely (file deleted, no alias, no shim — AC4) and make `/sdlc:docs` the single ADR surface: `/sdlc:docs seed adr "<pattern>"` seeds a founder-known ADR inline and `/sdlc:docs distill ["<focus>"]` distills the learnings corpus into promotable ADR candidates — both routing to the **retained** `refs/adr-pipeline.md` with the founder-confirm gate hoisted to the `/sdlc:docs` command layer. After this story all five `/sdlc:docs` modes are live and the future-mode stub tier is empty.

**Architecture:** A **plugin-authoring / command-absorption** story — it writes/deletes Markdown instructions, not code, and adds **no new pipeline** and **no new `docs/**` data**. The removal edit-set is **DERIVED from two grep families, never enumerated by line number** (see Global Constraints) — hand-enumerated line lists were the root cause of the spec's 10 review findings and must not be reintroduced. `refs/adr-pipeline.md` is retained as the single-source pipeline but is **not untouched**: it absorbs the relocated command-layer flow + branch/PR naming, gains the first-ADR base case, and re-points its own `/sdlc:adr` prose. `commands/docs.md` gains two live routes (`seed adr`, `distill`), the relocated empty-pattern STOP, and the reachability strikes. `commands/adr.md` is **deleted**. The `knowledge-engineer` ADR dispatch branch stays but **re-keys its selector on mode+type**, not command name. Everything else is a grep-A rewire (`/sdlc:adr` → `/sdlc:docs`) or a grep-B residue strike.

**Tech Stack:** Markdown (plugin instructions), `grep -rn` (the authoritative edit-set derivation), Prettier (CI-enforced on `plugins/**`; NA-62 non-idempotency landmine on 2-space list-nested fenced blocks), `check-agent-skill-preloads.sh` (CI guard), `jq` (plugin.json).
